Service terms

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Service

The Website Revenue Leak Audit is an advisory review of one customer-provided website. Delivery is targeted within 48 hours after both successful payment and complete intake details are received. If additional information is needed, the delivery clock begins when that information arrives.

What you receive

You receive a prioritized report covering conversion paths, mobile usability, trust, local search, and visible technical issues. The audit provides recommendations, not a guarantee of revenue, rankings, leads, or business outcomes.

Customer responsibility

You must have authority to request review of the submitted website and provide accurate contact and business information. The audit does not authorize ByQuill to change your website.

Refunds and cancellation

Refund policy: request cancellation before work begins for a full refund. After work begins, refunds are considered when ByQuill cannot deliver the agreed audit. Contact cole@byquill.co.

Scope

The introductory $49 price covers the audit report only. Implementation, ongoing monitoring, and custom consulting require separate written scope and pricing.
