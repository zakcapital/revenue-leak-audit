# Outreach Playbook

## Who to contact

Choose owner-operated service businesses whose websites show one visible, specific issue:

- no tap-to-call action on mobile
- unclear service area
- generic first-screen copy
- broken or overly long contact form
- missing title/description in search results
- slow or unreadable mobile layout

Do not imply that a flaw is costing a precise dollar amount unless there is evidence.

## First message

> Hi [Name] — I noticed [specific, verifiable issue] on [Business]’s website. On mobile, that makes it harder for someone to [desired action]. I put together focused website audits that prioritize fixes by likely customer impact. The introductory audit is $49 and delivered within 48 hours: [landing-page URL]. If useful, reply “audit” and I’ll send the three details I need.

## Follow-up after 3–4 business days

> One quick follow-up on the website note below. I’m happy to send a screenshot of the issue I found before you decide—no obligation.

## Qualification reply

> Great. Please send: 1) the website URL, 2) the service you most want inquiries for, and 3) whether the ideal action is a call, form, or booking. Delivery is within 48 hours after payment.

## Guardrails

- Maximum one follow-up.
- No fabricated urgency, traffic claims, or customer-loss estimates.
- Do not contact businesses that clearly prohibit solicitation.
- Personalize every first message with a real observation.
